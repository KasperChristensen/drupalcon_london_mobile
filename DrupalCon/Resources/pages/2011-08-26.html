Sprints

The traditional post-conference sprint, which will be held on Friday 26th August from 8:00 am to 6:00 pm, is an opportunity for anyone to pitch in and help make Drupal better, whether it's by writing code, reviewing patches, developing new modules and themes, or helping improve content and documentation on the Drupal.org website.

Sprints will be in the Fairfield Halls, Arnhem Gallery.
